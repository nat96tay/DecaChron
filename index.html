DecaChron Calendar

The DecaChron Calendar divides the year into 10 Megas alternating between 36 and 37 days. Each week, or Kilo, also consists of 10 days.

Every four years we have an additional day at the end of the year. (We skip the extra day every 100 years - but not every 400 years)
